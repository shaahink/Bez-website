## Voice & terminology (artist feedback, 16 Jul 2026 — follow exactly)

- Say **interdisciplinary** / **cross-disciplinary**.
- Say **sound**, not "music", when describing the practice. Exceptions: the
  fixed label **"music composition and analysis"**, and historical names
  (e.g. the award "Best Theatrical Music").
- Say **partners**, never "collaborators".
- Feel: **comforting, minimalistic** — like Max Richter's *Sleep* (which used
  432 Hz), like Brian Eno's color washes. Reference site he likes:
  <https://roberthenke.com>. Slow motion, long fades, nothing loud.
- **Hero: no photo of Bruce.** Artwork slideshow with a noise/grain dissolve,
  no visible slideshow controls, merged with the page (not a separate widget).

## Project facts (verified against the CVs — do not embellish)

- **Wordless** — 2025, Colleague Complex Gallery (a.k.a. Colleague Space),
  Shiraz. Inaugural exhibition of the gallery. Action calligraphy, live sound
  installation, video projection. Roles: curator, art director, sound designer.
  Team of 5. ~400 visitors over four nights. Soundscapes across 14 calligraphic
  works. Recognition from artists Alireza Tal-Jangani and Majid Aslami.
- **Forgotten** ("Archive of Dispersed Futures") — July 1–2, 2025, Dalan
  Restoration Studio, Shiraz. Site-specific sound installation in a 200-year-old
  Qajar-era house: nine Persian mystical words whose meanings eroded, spatialised
  sound + action calligraphy + architectural intervention. Roles: curator, art
  director, sound designer. Team of 15. ~200 attendees over two nights. Five
  site-specific compositions.
- **Mirror** — 2024 MA final project, AUB: audio-visual installation on
  identity, repetition, randomness. Innovation Prize. Also **Mirror (album)** —
  9 electroacoustic compositions released on Spotify/Apple Music (2025–26).
- **Aequilibrium** — 2024, spudWORKS Open ("Spudopen"), Sway, New Forest —
  audio-visual work on balance and release. Highly Commended Artist.
- **Anima, the inner life** — 2025, spudWORKS Open — interactive audiovisual
  installation on interiority and presence (photo: screen + calligraphy scroll,
  `images/showcase/anima.jpg`).
- **Rhinoplasty** — 2023, MA Fine Art Symposium, Anglia Ruskin, Cambridge —
  site-specific sound installation (spoken word, mirror, body image).
- **Cities and Memory** — autumn 2025 — accepted field-recording/composition
  contribution to the international sound-mapping project.
- **Two** — 2019, solo, Honar Cinema and Gallery, Shiraz — audio-visual
  installation on duality and transformation.
- Film & stage sound (selected): *Amiroo* (2022, short documentary — Best Short
  Documentary, Seoul Int'l Film Festival 2023; also screened Echo Briks 2024),
  *Gavazn* (2022), *The Pledge* (2022), *Ramak Industry* (2022), *closed-eye*
  (2021), *Recursion* (2018), *Angel's Smile* (2017), *Dandelion* (2015),
  *Zaar* (2013, theater — Best Theatrical Music, Fajr Festival of Fars),
  *Of the World, Dust Remains* (2019, stage work by Roozbeh Tabandeh).
- Work: **Art director, Bimo Axis** (London, 2025–present); freelance sound
  designer & electronic composer (2020–present); sound consultant, AUB Film
  Practice MA (2023–24); recording studio assistant & foley, Audio Engineering
  Academy, Tehran (2021–23); event manager & art consultant, Honar Cinema and
  Gallery, Shiraz (2017–20) — 30+ exhibitions and events.
- **Klezmeri Ensemble**, Shiraz (2017–present) — band leader, violinist,
  arranger; Balkan & Klezmer repertoire; Nowruz festival commissions (2021,
  2022 at Shahr Complex / Learak residency).
- Teaching (2025, Shiraz): social history of music; contemporary history of art
  through photography (Forough & Mahour institutes); Eastern European music
  workshop. Current research: *Sonic Textiles* (Persian modes ↔ textile
  patterns) and *The Sound of Identity* (heritage-site soundscapes).

## Site structure (since July 2026 rework)

- **index.html** — hero (artwork slideshow, noise dissolve, `js/hero.js`) →
  marquee → statement → about (short, links to about.html) → works chapters
  (Forgotten, Wordless — data in `js/main.js`) → showcase tease band →
  partners grid → moving image → contact (email only) → footer.
- **showcase.html** — the calligraphy gallery. Presented as the result of
  Bruce's direction/curation ("art manager" wording is banned); calligraphy
  credited to Behnam. Three rooms: I Canvases, II In the open, III Works on
  paper; crimson interlude; project notes (Wordless/Forgotten facts above);
  closing contact note. Lightbox = PhotoSwipe via `.gallery`/`.gallery-item`.
- **about.html** — consolidated from all seven CVs: statement, practice areas,
  exhibitions, sound for film & stage, experience, education & awards,
  recordings, teaching & research.
- Design system in `css/styles.css` ("The Listening Room" + the 2026
  "comforting" layer: slow Eno-style color wash, hero dissolve, rooms).
