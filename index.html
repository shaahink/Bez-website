Artist Statement

Repetition is a myth — each iteration carries the temporal signature of its moment.

What masquerades as return is actually departure; what appears cyclical reveals itself as spiral. Time itself ensures that no gesture, no sound, no form can truly repeat, for difference inhabits the very core of what we mistake for sameness.

Bruce Nemeth

About the artist

I am an interdisciplinary artist living and working in the UK — art director and curator, weaving installation and sound into cross-disciplinary, multisensory explorations.

My art is rooted in the choreography of internal behaviours — where repetition and randomness shape reflective environments and sonic encounters. Each project invites immersion in chance, memory, and self: an attention paid to the small departures that live inside every act of returning.
