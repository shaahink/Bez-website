About

I am an interdisciplinary artist living and working in the UK, weaving curation, installation, and sound into multisensory explorations. My art is rooted in the choreography of internal behaviors—where repetition and randomness shape reflective environments and sonic encounters. Each project invites immersion in chance, memory, and self.

Artist Statement

Repetition is a myth—each iteration carries the temporal signature of its moment, contaminated by the weight of its own becoming. What masquerades as return is actually departure; what appears cyclical reveals itself as spiral. Time itself ensures that no gesture, no sound, no form can truly repeat, for difference inhabits the very core of what we mistake for sameness.
